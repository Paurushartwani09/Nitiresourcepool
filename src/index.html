How to Apply?
Joining the NITI Aayog Resource Pool is your first step towards making a significant impact on India's development. Follow these simple steps to get started:
1.Create an Account
Start Your Journey: Begin by creating your account on the NITI Aayog Resource Pool portal. Click on the Join button and enter your basic information. You will receive an email to verify your account, ensuring secure access to our portal.
2. Complete your profile
Showcase Your Potential: After logging in, complete your profile by providing your educational qualifications and professional experience. This detailed information will help us understand your background and expertise better. Ensure that all information is accurate and up-to-date to maximize your chances of being matched with suitable opportunities.
3.Get Notification for Interview
Once your profile is complete, our recruitment team will review it against available positions. If your skills and experience align with our requirements, you will be shortlisted for further assessment. You will receive notifications about job openings that match your profile, keeping you informed about potential opportunities to join NITI Aayog’s mission-driven workforce.
